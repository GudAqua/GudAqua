Gud Aqua

In Gud Aqua, we believe in our guiding principle: "We Love Life, We Treat Water Right." Since its founding in 2019, Gud Aqua Purified and Mineral Water Refilling Station has consistently provided quality water products and services. We ensure your safety by providing High-Quality product water through 24 stages filtration system, and the source of water is NAWASA, which is in itself free of contaminants like bacteria, microbes and pesticides. At the moment, our TDS is 1mg/L. acceptable value is 10 mg/L. We are compliant with and have "PASSED" Results every six months for Water Testing as required and conducted by Dept of Health. The water tests include Bacterial, Physical, and Micro-organisms. The Staff are trained and follow the health and safety protocol we have set, use PPE in the production area and toilet facilities for hygiene and have a high standard of cleanliness of the WRS. The Staff is having and passed the annual health check, including the Hepa B Testing, among others


Quality Service:

We have the most efficient way of receiving and delivering orders. Staff are trained, reminded and observed to be polite and respectful to the customers and to each other. The Staff and the Management welcome comments and suggestions from customers for the improvement of our services. The Management is always objective in dealing with Staff and customers' conflicts and issues.


Social Relevance:

As part of our mission and goals, Gud Aqua WRS wanted to be viewed as proactive in community development and responsive to social issues. We welcome working students to help them gain life skills and financial benefits. We have financially donated to Pilot School during the COVID-19 pandemic, sponsoring water supplies to various RUH and LGU events. We have provided financial assistance to our Staff to have their own home, medical assistance and scholarships.

Our Vision:

Gud Aqua Purified and Mineral Water Refilling System is the leading and trusted company in providing the highest quality product water that will alleviate water-borne disease and improve quality of life.

Our Mission:

  • Treat the water right from source to customers
  • Continuously find ways to improve the quality and quantity of our product water and the quality of our services by being proactive and objective.
  • Collaborate with local authorities, organisations, groups and individuals in promoting and enhancing the quality of life, especially that of the children and vulnerable, sick sector of society.
  • The staff and the customers are proud of our product and services.
  • The staff is proud to work with Gud Aqua, where they have enhanced their quality of life, gained life skills, and experienced financial growth.
